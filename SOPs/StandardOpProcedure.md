## **üìç 3. ON-SITE PROCEDURES**

### 3.1 Arrival & Setup

* Arrive 10-15 mins early
* Knock/ring doorbell or notify business staff
* Walk the job site with the client (if available)
* Confirm:

  * Access to water and electrical outlets
  * Any fragile items to protect (plants, cameras, etc.)

### 3.2 Surface Preparation

* Remove debris (sweep/blow with leaf blower)
* Cover any electrical outlets, AC units, light fixtures
* Apply pre-treatment (if needed):

  * Mold/mildew: 3:1 Water + Sodium Hypochlorite
  * Oil: Degreaser
  * Rust: Rust remover (use per instructions)

### 3.3 Execution by Surface Type

#### 3.3.1 **Driveways / Patios / Sidewalks**

* Use surface cleaner in overlapping passes
* Rinse edges with fan-tip nozzle
* Post-treat with SH solution if mildew remains

#### 3.3.2 **Siding (Residential/Commercial)**

* Use soft wash (low-pressure + cleaning solution)
* Apply bottom-up, rinse top-down
* Avoid forcing water behind siding or into vents

#### 3.3.3 **Roofs**

* ONLY soft wash for shingles or tile
* Do not use high-pressure on roofing
* Use pump sprayer or dedicated soft wash pump

#### 3.3.4 **Gutters**

* Remove debris manually or with gutter vacuum
* Flush with hose to ensure downspouts are clear
* Optional: Install gutter guards (upsell opportunity)

#### 3.3.5 **Trash Compactors / Dumpster Pads**

* Spray degreaser and let dwell 5-10 mins
* Use hot water pressure washer + surface cleaner
* Rinse surrounding area thoroughly

#### 3.3.6 **Shopping Cart Areas / Playgrounds**

* Use food-safe sanitizer (e.g., Vital Oxide)
* Avoid aggressive pressure around kids‚Äô equipment
* Use medium-pressure rinse to finish

#### 3.3.7 **Parking Lots / Drive-Thrus**

* Sweep or blow debris before washing
* Use surface cleaner + wand for edges
* Post-treat gum spots or oil with degreaser

### 3.4 Final Walkthrough

* Walk client through completed job
* Take before/after photos for portfolio
* Send job completion form or invoice (via Jobber/QBO)
* Ask for review on Google/Yelp (provide QR code or link)
