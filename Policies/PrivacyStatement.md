## 3) Personal Information We Collect

We collect information directly from you, automatically from your device/browser, and from service providers we use to run our business (e.g., payment processors, calendar, email/SMS, analytics).

### 3.1 Information You Provide

* **Contact details:** name, email, phone, address (service location & billing).
* **Service details:** property type, requested services, photos/videos you upload for estimates.
* **Scheduling details:** preferred dates/times, availability, job notes.
* **Payment info:** if you pay online we use a PCI-compliant processor (e.g., **Stripe**)—we **do not** store your full card number. If you pay via **Cash App** or other methods, we receive limited payment confirmations/metadata.
* **Communications:** emails, SMS, phone call notes, chat form messages, and support requests.
* **Consent records:** e.g., email/SMS marketing opt-in/out, cookie preferences, signature approvals.

### 3.2 Information We Collect Automatically

* **Device/technical data:** IP address, browser type, OS, language, referring/exit pages, timestamps, page views, approximate location (derived from IP).
* **Cookies & similar tech:** see **Section 8**.
* **Security/anti-fraud signals:** rate-limiting, error logs, request headers.

### 3.3 Information From Service Providers & Integrations

* **Payments:** Stripe (transaction status, last 4 of card, brand, amount, currency, failure reasons).
* **Email/SMS & notifications:** message status (sent/delivered/bounced).
* **Calendaring/booking (if enabled):** appointment confirmations/updates.
* **Forms (Google Forms / site forms):** responses stored in our systems or linked Google Sheets.
* **Maps/Geocoding (Google Maps):** for service area and routing.

> We do **not** collect or process **biometric** identifiers, nor do we intentionally collect **sensitive** data (e.g., SSN, precise GPS location) unless strictly necessary (and only with your consent or as legally required).

## 7) Data Retention

We keep Personal Information only as long as necessary for the purposes above, including:

* **Operational records:** typically up to **7 years** for tax/audit/bookkeeping (or longer if legally required).
* **Communications & scheduling:** while your account/relationship is active and for a reasonable time after for records and customer service.
* **Marketing preferences:** until you opt out or the program ends.
* **Logs/security data:** typically short-lived unless needed for investigations.

When no longer needed, we delete or de-identify data according to our retention schedule and legal obligations.

## 8) Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use minimal cookies and similar technologies to:

* Keep the site working (session, security, CSRF protection, load balancing/CDN).
* Understand basic usage (page views, errors) to improve reliability.
* Remember preferences (e.g., cookie banner choices).

**Controls:** You can adjust browser settings to block/clear cookies. Blocking required cookies may break site features. We do **not** honor “Do Not Track” (DNT) signals due to a lack of standardization but we strive to minimize tracking.

## 9) Security

We implement administrative, technical, and physical safeguards:

* HTTPS/TLS in transit; restricted data access; role-based permissions.
* Least-privilege accounts; password hygiene; MFA where possible.
* Regular updates and security patches; provider compliance (e.g., Stripe’s PCI-DSS).
  No system is 100% secure. Please protect your devices and promptly notify us of any suspected unauthorized activity.

## 16) Definitions

* **Personal Information (PI):** Information that identifies, relates to, describes, or can reasonably be linked to an individual or household.
* **Processing:** Any operation performed on PI (collection, use, storage, disclosure, deletion).
* **Service Providers/Processors:** Third parties that process PI on our behalf under contract.
* **Sale/Share (state law):** Disclosing PI for monetary value or cross-context behavioral advertising. *We do neither.*
