## Purpose of SMS Communication

This document certifies that customers of **A & W Pressure Washing and Cleaning** have provided **express consent** to receive automated text messages (SMS) from our business through our communications provider, **Twilio**, for the following purposes:

- Appointment confirmations and reminders  
- Schedule updates or rescheduling notices  
- Service follow-ups and feedback requests  
- Notifications related to billing or estimates  
- Emergency or weather-related rescheduling updates  

---

## Consent Collection Method

Customers provide consent to receive text messages through one or more of the following methods:

1. **Online Appointment Form Submission** – Customers fill out a scheduling form on our official website with a consent statement visible near the submission button.  
2. **Verbal Consent** – Staff record verbal consent during scheduling calls or in-person interactions.  
3. **Written Consent** – Signed estimate forms or service agreements include the SMS consent clause.  
4. **Existing Customer Relationship** – Returning customers have consented via previous interactions consistent with TCPA guidelines.  

---

## Opt-Out Policy

Customers can opt out at any time by replying **STOP** to any text message.  
Once opted out, their phone number is automatically added to our **Do Not Contact list** maintained in our system and Twilio’s suppression list.
